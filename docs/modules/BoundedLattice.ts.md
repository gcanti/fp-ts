## BoundedLattice overview

A `BoundedLattice` must satisfy the following in addition to `BoundedMeetSemilattice` and `BoundedJoinSemilattice` laws:

- Absorption law for meet: `a ∧ (a ∨ b) <-> a`
- Absorption law for join: `a ∨ (a ∧ b) <-> a`

Added in v2.0.0
